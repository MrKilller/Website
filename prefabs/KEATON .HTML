!!!!!!INTRODUCTION TO KEATON ELLIS!!!!!!

Keaton Ellis is an American speedcuber. He got a 5.09 3x3 single in 2015, which wasn't officially recognized as a world record as Lucas Etter achieved a 4.90 single at the same competition during the next round.

Keaton Ellis is born and is currently living in USA.He is currently studing in USA high school as a student as well as a teacher(teaching cubes).he is been one of the best speed cube solvers in the mid 1990's.

Keaton has got his record of 5.09 secs (Solve video available below) in the Second Round and that was the record for then. (Previous record was Collin Burns - 5.25 secs) But in the Semi Finals, Lucas broke that record by solving in 4.90 seconds(Solve video available below). So, Even though Keaton might have held the record for a couple of hours, at the end of the day, it is Etter's record.
